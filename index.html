Silent Step — A discreet way to get help when you can't speak.

In some situations, calling for help isn't safe. Silent Step is designed for that moment.

📞 1800RESPECT: 1800 737 732  (24/7 National Helpline)
The Problem

When Calling Isn’t Safe

In high-risk situations, people are not always able to call or speak safely. Attempting to use a phone may increase danger, and in many cases, no alert is sent at all. This means no one knows help is needed.

The Solution

A Silent Alert That Works

Silent Step allows a user to trigger an emergency alert discreetly. With a hidden action, the app sends an urgent message with the user's location to trusted contacts. Those contacts are clearly instructed to contact emergency services immediately.

Safety Notice

Silent Step does not replace emergency services. Always call 000 directly when it is safe to do so. This app is designed for situations where calling or speaking is not possible or may increase risk.

How It Works

Four Steps to Safety

Silent Step is designed for situations where speaking or calling is not possible. Here is how it works.

1
👁️

Trigger Discreetly

Activate via a hidden button sequence or floating button without drawing attention.

2
📍

Your precise location is recorded instantly in the background.

3
📨

Alert Sent

An emergency message with your location is sent to your trusted contacts.

4
👤

Contacts Act

Your contacts are clearly instructed to call 000 immediately on your behalf.

Why It Matters

Silent Step is designed for situations where communication is not possible. It creates a way for someone to reach out for help without speaking, explaining, or drawing attention. That difference can matter.
